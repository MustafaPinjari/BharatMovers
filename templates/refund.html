Refund & Cancellation Policy

Our Refund & Cancellation Policy outlines the process and conditions under which you can request a refund or cancel services. We aim to provide fair and transparent guidelines for all our customers.

Cancellation Policy

  • Order Timing/Status: Cancellations can be done before an order is shipped and/or prior to start of service. After engagement, a cancellation fee may apply.
  • Third-Party Services: Cancellations for solutions (such as domain registration or third-party digital goods) are subject to those third partyâ€™s refund and cancellation terms. (See your confirmation/receipt for specifics.)
  • Virtual/Downloadable: You cannot cancel services once work is 50% or more fulfilled, delivered, or downloadable (e.g. canceled order is valid if less than 50% is delivered or access has not been sent).

Refund Policy

  • When Refunds Happen: Refunds for services that have not been charged for or where objectives will not be processed in 2-5 more business days. Please contact support within 24 hours of purchase if you believe you are eligible for a refund.
  • Refund Method: Refunds will be credited to the original payment method, minus processing fees where applicable. Refunds for completed services are not issued. Requests must be made within 24 hours from the date of order.
  • No Partial Refunds: We do not provide partial refunds for unused portions of partially completed services unless otherwise stated on your order invoice or contract.

How to Request a Refund or Cancellation

To request a service refund or a cancellation, please contact support team at Email or Phone Number. Provide your order booking details, reason for cancellation, and any relevant information to help process your request quickly.

Processing Time

Refunds will be processed within 2-7 business days, depending on the payment method used. Refunds to credit/debit cards may take longer due to bank processing timelines.

Non-refundable Services

Certain services are not eligible for a refund, including:

  • Domain registration
  • Digital downloads
  • Completed services

Dispute Resolution

If you are dissatisfied with a refund or cancellation decision, you can escalate the issue to our customer service team leader. Further escalation steps will be shared if the matter isn't resolved to your satisfaction.

Changes to Refund & Cancellation Policy

We reserve the right to update or change this policy at any time. Any changes will be notified via customer email, and continued use of services implies your acceptance of the updated policy.
